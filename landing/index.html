How It Works

Three steps. One read-only connection.

01 CONNECT

Read-only. Your data stays where it is.

VEKTOR connects to your existing platform via API — identity provider, ERP, or any enterprise system. It reads entities, entitlements, and events. Nothing is moved, copied, or stored permanently.

→
02 GENERATE

ML models generate signals continuously.

A growing library of models scores everything — humans, AI agents, bots, service accounts. Peer deviation, anomaly detection, delegation inheritance, cross-domain amplification, and more.

→
03 CONSUME

Your agents call the API and act.

One REST API. Structured JSON signal packages. Each signal includes confidence, recommended action, rollback payload, and feature attribution. Your agent reads, decides, and acts.

VEKTOR doesn't replace anything in your stack. It reads from the systems you already have and generates intelligence your agents can't get anywhere else. Adding a new data source means writing one adapter — zero model changes.
